# FuneralPress Screen Recording Video Guide

## Overview

10 screen recording videos for X/Twitter ads. Record on the live site, edit in CapCut (free), pair with existing tweet kit.

---

## Recording Tips

- **Use your phone** in landscape mode on the live site (feels more authentic for X)
- **Slow, deliberate taps** — don't rush, let viewers follow along
- **Clean state** — use fresh/demo data, no personal info visible
- **Screen record tool**: Built-in (iPhone/Android) or OBS on desktop

## CapCut Edit Template (for each video)

```
[0:00-0:02]  Black screen + text: "FuneralPress"
[0:02-0:XX]  Screen recording with subtle zoom-ins on key actions
[last 3s]    End card: "Try free → funeralpress.org" + logo
```

- **Music**: CapCut free royalty-free tracks — search "gentle piano" or "soft ambient"
- **Text style**: White text, slight shadow, bottom-third placement
- **Speed**: 1.2-1.5x on boring parts (loading screens, typing)

## Posting Strategy

Pair each video with the matching tweet from the social-media-kit. Use videos for the morning slot (higher engagement) and static images for the evening slot.

| Video | Pair with Tweet Kit Day | Slot |
|-------|------------------------|------|
| Brochure editor | Day 1 | Morning |
| Memorial page | Day 3 | Morning |
| Budget planner | Day 4 | Morning |
| One-week card | Day 5 | Morning |
| Guest book | Day 6 | Morning |
| Photo gallery | Day 7 | Morning |
| Obituary creator | Day 8 | Morning |
| WhatsApp share | Day 13 | Evening |
| Partner dashboard | Day 23 | Morning |
| Full overview | Day 30 | Morning |

Use the **static image** from the kit for the other slot that day. Videos get priority in X's algorithm — morning posts typically see higher reach.
