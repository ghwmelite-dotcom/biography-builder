Inside of Card

A Note of Gratitude

◆

Words cannot adequately express how deeply grateful we are for your kindness, love, and support during this most difficult time in our lives.

Your presence at the funeral of our beloved mother, grandmother, and wife, Grace Amara Johnson, brought us immeasurable comfort. Every prayer whispered, every hand held, every tear shared reminded us that we are not alone in our grief.

Mom always said that love is the greatest gift we can give one another. Through your compassion, you have given us that gift in abundance. She would have been so moved by the outpouring of love from her community.

May God bless you richly for your generosity of spirit, and may the love you have shown our family return to you a hundredfold.

◆

The Johnson Family

Robert, Samuel, Elizabeth, Kwame Jr. & Abena
