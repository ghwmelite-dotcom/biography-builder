# Stream 5: Growth Hacking & Viral Mechanics

## 5A. Referral & Word-of-Mouth

### Zero-Budget Referral Programme: "Honour Someone, Help Someone"

**Concept:** Every family that creates a memorial or brochure can unlock premium features by sharing the platform. No cash incentives — the currency is access.

**How it works:**
1. After first design, user sees: "Share FuneralPress with another family and unlock premium themes for free."
2. Each user gets a unique referral link.
3. When a referred person creates an account and starts a design, the referrer earns one "Honour Credit."
4. Credits unlock: premium themes (1), extra memorial features (2), priority WhatsApp support (3).

### 5 Natural Share Triggers

| Trigger | Why They Share | What to Show |
|---------|---------------|-------------|
| First brochure preview | Pride — "Look what I made for Mama" | Share button with preview image |
| Memorial page goes live | Need to distribute link | WhatsApp share pre-loaded with message |
| QR code generation | Feels modern and impressive | Shareable QR image |
| Budget planner completion | Relief and accomplishment | Shareable summary card |
| After the funeral (day 3-7) | Gratitude phase | Automated message: "Your memorial has been viewed X times" |

### Social Proof Strategy

**Collect:** Single-question feedback after every design. Screenshot positive WhatsApp messages.
**Display:** Homepage counter ("Join 500+ families"), rotating testimonials, X posts about milestones.
**Amplify:** Memorial page footer: "Created with FuneralPress." Brochure PDF includes "Designed with FuneralPress.org." Hashtag: #FuneralPressGH.

### Newsjacking Rules

1. Never exploit a death for marketing. Always lead with empathy.
2. Only insert yourself when genuinely relevant.
3. Offer value, not a sales pitch.
4. Be fast — within 2-4 hours of a trend emerging.
