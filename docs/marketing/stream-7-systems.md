## 7C. Weekly Routine (Under 1 Hour/Day)

| Day | Tasks | Time |
|---|---|---|
| **Monday** | Review last week's analytics. Plan 5 social posts. Write & schedule 2 in Buffer. | 40 min |
| **Tuesday** | Write 1 blog post or content piece. Reply to all comments/DMs. Share 1 post in a group. | 45 min |
| **Wednesday** | Schedule remaining 3 posts. Send 3 DM outreach messages. Check Search Console. | 40 min |
| **Thursday** | Engage on 5 high-traffic posts. Repurpose blog into 2 formats. Update tracking sheet. | 40 min |
| **Friday** | Send 1 journalist outreach email. Check user feedback. Answer 1 Quora/Reddit question. | 35 min |
| **Saturday** | Create 2-3 visual assets for next week. Browse trending topics. | 30 min |
| **Sunday** | Review the week. Plan adjustments. Optional: draft next blog outline. | 30 min |

**Total: ~4.5 hours/week (~40 min/day average)**
